Privacy Policy

At Outlet Expense, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our inventory management system.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Outlet Expense, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details such as name, email address, and contact information.
  • Business information including company name, business transactions, and inventory details.
  • Technical data like your IP address, browser type, and system logs for security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used to improve your experience and ensure the smooth functioning of Outlet Expense, including:

  • Managing your account and providing access to our services.
  • Processing transactions and managing inventory records.
  • Enhancing system features based on usage patterns and feedback.
  • Ensuring system security and preventing unauthorized access.

3. Data Sharing & Protection

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • If required by law or legal processes.
  • To protect the rights and safety of Outlet Expense and its users.
  • When partnering with trusted service providers who help operate our platform.

We implement strong security measures to protect your data, including encryption and access control.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@outletexpense.com.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed.

Last updated: October 2024

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at privacy@outletexpense.com.
