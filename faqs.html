Frequently Asked Questions

What is Outlet Expense?

Outlet Expense is an advanced inventory management system that helps businesses track and manage products, expenses, and profits efficiently.

How does the profit calculation work?

Our system calculates profit for both product types with and without variants, even if they appear in the same transaction.

Can I manage multiple payments and dues?

Yes, Outlet Expense supports multi-payment systems and due collection management for customers.

Is support available for my organization?

Yes, we provide full technical support to ensure your organization operates smoothly with our system.

Is there a trial period for Outlet Expense?

Yes, we offer a free trial period for new users to explore the features and benefits of Outlet Expense before making a commitment.

How can I integrate Outlet Expense with my current systems?

Outlet Expense provides API access and documentation to ensure smooth integration with your existing systems, such as accounting or e-commerce platforms.

Does Outlet Expense handle inventory across multiple locations?

Yes, Outlet Expense allows you to track inventory levels across multiple store locations and warehouses in real-time.

How often is my data backed up?

We ensure automatic daily backups for all your data, safeguarding against any potential loss.

Can I customize the reports generated by Outlet Expense?

Yes, Outlet Expense provides customizable reporting tools that allow you to generate specific reports based on your business needs.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for our subscription plans.
